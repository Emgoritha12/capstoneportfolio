Case Study: Marvel Fandom For one of my journalism classes, I was informed to do a case study on a group of people. I decided to do a case study on the marvel fandom and how they function as a community online and in person.

Revision: Conformity and Dimensions of Credibility as Persuasion

For my persuasion class, I was informed to do an assignment on a aspect of persuasion that was discussed in class. I decided to do conformity as a form of persuasion. I was also later instructed to do a revision to this assignment, so I made sure to add the dimensions of credibility in addition to conformity to show what makes a source credible
